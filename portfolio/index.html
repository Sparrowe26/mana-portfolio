Hello, and welcome to my portfolio!

I'm a game artist and designer with a passion for creating and telling a good story! My strengths lie in art and design, but I'm trained when it comes to programming as well. I've been the integral part of project teams where I strive to bridge the gap between the artistic and techinical sides of game-making. I think the best part is when everything comes together in the end to make something truly spectacular.

See below for the projects I've had the pleasure of working on!

Mushroom Kid's Big Grass Sword ★

Full production: September 2023 -> PRESENT
Game Jam: November 2022

Mushroom Kid’s Big Grass Sword is an award-winning physics-based precision platformer that follows Mica, a little mushroom, on a journey to save their village from a giant bird with a sword that grows!

  • My role: Lead Artist, Designer, Animator
    I make sure all of the game art is planned out, cohesive, and fits the unique style of the game. I also handle in-engine art implementation; UI, UX, and marketing art; as well as helped out with general level design and pacing.
  • Team size: Currently 5
  • Engine: Godot
  • Language: GDScript

Trampolinko

Duration: February -> May 2025

Trampolinko is a mobile Peggle-like arcade game where you draw trampolines on the screen that bounce the ball upwards into the pegs. With satisfying physics and many different peg layouts, strive for a high score!

  • My role: Artist/Technical Artist, UI/UX
    I created all the art for the game, designed the general layout of the gameplay and menus, and I worked on implementation.

Rock On, Raccoon!

Duration: May -> August 2023

Rock On, Raccoon! is a mobile rhythm game where you play as a raccoon garage band and battle other animal bands in order to become rock stars and reclaim your turf! Play through 5 different genres of music beat mapped at 4 different difficulties, and aim for a high score!

  • My role: Artist, Designer, Animator
    I was the team’s UI/UX artist, animator, and character designer. I also contributed to the design team by creating the Google Sheets system for planning and exporting beat maps with a very helpful system that made mapping easy to visualize.
  • Team size: 6
  • Engine: Unity
  • Language: C#
